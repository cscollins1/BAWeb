Notice of Intent (NOI)

Depending on the county your child lives in, you will need to submit a Notice of Intent to Provide Home Instruction (NOI) to the Home Instruction Specialist designated for your county. The website, HEAV.org, will prove to be insightful and can answer many questions in detail that you may have.
