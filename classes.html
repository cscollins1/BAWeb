Classes

Classes and their descriptions may be viewed in two different ways, either on the schedule or by subject

ON THE SCHEDULE

To view the schedule hover over "Schedule" on the toggle bar and then scroll down and select the schedule you wish to view, either Kindergarten - 6th grade OR 6th - 12th grade.

BY SUBJECT

To view our course offerings by subject, hover over "Classes" on the toggle bar and then scroll down and select the topic of the courses you wish to view.
