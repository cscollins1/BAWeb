How do I submit my Notice of Intent? (NOI)

  • Submit the Application to Interview form or email Emily Kirtlan at etkirtlan@gmail.com
  • Schedule and complete the required phone interview.
  • After the interview, if both parties feel we are a good fit for one another, you may email the registration form to Emily Kirtlan at etkirtlan@gmail.com or bridgeacademy@comcast.net
  • Three letters of references must be submitted on behalf of you/ your student; one must be from a pastor/ or leader in your church

Contact Bridge Academy at bridgeacademy@comcast.net
